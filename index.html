We aim to develop novel tools using genomic approaches for translational medicine in psychiatric disorders

We are a multidisciplinary group that includes psychiatrists, geneticists, bioinformaticians and engineers focused in improving the quality of life for people affected by psychiatric conditions.

Research

Brief summary of group objectives

Our group was born after the collaboration between Claudio Toma and Enrique Baca García in 2019. Claudio Toma PhD has studied for the last 20 years the genomic architecture of psychiatric diseases, whereas Enrique Baca García has made a great effort in contributing to personalized treatments to psychiatric patients.
Our expertise in high-throughput data analyses is helping us to design new and innovative diagnostic and treatment approaches to bipolar disorder, combining digital phenotyping, genomics and machine learning algorithms.
